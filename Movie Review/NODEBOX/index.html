My Name is Khan

Description

My Name Is Khan is a 2010 Indian drama film directed by Karan Johar, produced by Hiroo Yash Johar and Gauri Khan, and starring Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol in lead roles. The film was jointly produced by Dharma Productions and Red Chillies Entertainment and was distributed by FOX Star Entertainment, which had bought the rights for the film for a sum of ₹100 crore (US$14 million), making it the most expensive Bollywood film of 2010.

Cast

  • Shah Rukh Khan as Rizwan
  • Kajol as Mandira
  • Jimmy Shergill as Zakir Khan
  • Sonya Jehan as Haseena Khan
  • Arjan Aujla as Sameer Khan

Budget

The film is made with the budget of ₹450–600 million and has earned about ₹2.23 billion. The movie is considered as a hit.

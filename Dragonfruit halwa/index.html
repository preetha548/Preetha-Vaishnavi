🍨 Dragon Fruit Halwa

🧾 Ingredients

  • 2 cups dragon fruit pulp
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • Cashews & almonds
  • Cardamom powder

👩‍🍳 Preparation Steps

  1. Mash the dragon fruit pulp.
  2. Mix cornflour with milk.
  3. Heat ghee and fry nuts.
  4. Cook pulp for 5–7 minutes.
  5. Add sugar and stir well.
  6. Add cornflour mixture slowly.
  7. Cook until thick and glossy.
